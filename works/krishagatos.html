— About

Krishagatos is the debut album of the electronic-improvisation trio .jaArana+Ф. Using experimental live electronics, the group explores evolving textures, noise layers, and spectral interplay through fully improvised, technology-driven performance.

Nothing is composed in advance. Each piece emerges from the real-time negotiation of three independent electronic voices: materials accumulate, collide, and dissolve according to the logic of the moment. The result is a music that resists repetition, where the act of listening and the act of playing share the same condition of irreversibility.

Recorded in Geneva, the album documents a single session that captures the trio at the intersection of noise, texture, and improvisational rigor.
