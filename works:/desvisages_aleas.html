Album — 2026

Des Visages
— aleas

The second recording project by Des Visages, recorded at Dijktheater (Amsterdam) in March 2026. The title derives from the Latin alea ("dice" or "chance"), reflecting the project's focus on unpredictability and real-time decision-making.

The album features three improvisations—alea II, alea III, and alea V—where musical decisions unfold entirely in the moment. The recording was accompanied by three video works documenting the duo's exploration of lighting and real-time video projection, with light programmed to behave randomly in speed, intensity, and color.
